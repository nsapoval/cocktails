A quick and easy passion fruit syrup from Smuggler's Cove.

---

* 1 cup [passion fruit puree](https://amzn.to/4cgMiRy)
* 1 cup 2:1 syrup

Combine the liquids and stir well, will keep in the fridge for 2 to 3 weeks.
