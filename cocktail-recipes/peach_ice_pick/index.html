Bourbon Peach Basil Ice Pick

Created in June 29, 2024

2024   ·   sour   bourbon   fruity

The original recipe is by McKenzie Nelson from Ranger Station, Atlanta, GA via Imbibe Magazine.

  • 2 oz peach-infused bourbon
  • 1.5 oz brewed black tea
  • 0.75 oz peach syrup
  • 0.75 oz lemon juice
  • 0.25 oz Amaro Montenegro
  • 5 fresh basil leaves

Shake all ingredients with ice and double strain into a rocks glass over a single large cube. Garnish with a lemon peel and a basil leaf.

The cocktail fairs fine without the basil for a less complex drink reminiscent of a spiked peach iced tea. I used Taiwanese Dongfan meiren tea brewed strong. The tea should be quite bold to stand up to the rest of the flavors, so I recommend slightly oversteeping it.
